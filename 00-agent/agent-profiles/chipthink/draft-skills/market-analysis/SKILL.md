# Market Analysis

Use when evaluating whether an app, product, or campaign is worth pursuing.

## Workflow

1. Define market category and target user.
2. Identify direct and adjacent competitors.
3. Review pricing, positioning, reviews, features, and distribution channels.
4. Look for underserved workflows, weak UX, pricing gaps, or fast-growing niches.
5. Recommend a positioning and validation path.

## Output

- Market summary.
- Competitor landscape.
- User pain signals.
- Differentiation opportunities.
- Monetization options.
- Validation tests.
- Risks and red flags.
