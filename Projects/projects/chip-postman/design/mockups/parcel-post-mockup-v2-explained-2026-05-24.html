What The Player Does

1. Start at the Post Office

The yellow PO tile is the home base. This is where the red postman collects parcels.

2. Pick up the parcel

The brown PKG tile shows the next parcel. Once collected, the destination lights up.

3. Deliver to the highlighted house

The yellow outlined cottage is the target. Reaching it completes the delivery and adds score.

4. Avoid cute hazards

Puddles slow the postman down. Later hazards could be cats, geese, bikes, muddy shortcuts, or dogs.

5. Beat the clock

The timer counts down to sunset. More successful deliveries before sunset means a better end-of-day grade.

6. Chase better runs

The dopamine loop is score, streaks, stamps, happy villager reactions, and trying again for an A or S grade.
