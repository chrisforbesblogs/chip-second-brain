## Product Scope From Transcript

LifeApp is intended as a consumer health super app combining:

- Fitness tracking
- Habit tracking
- Macro and micronutrient tracking
- Food photo recognition, barcode scanning, and food database lookup
- Water intake and daily health goals
- Sleep and recovery dashboard
- Garmin, Whoop, Wahoo, and similar wearable/device integrations
- Strava-like social layer
- Atomic Habits-inspired habit-building education and reminders
- Later AI personal trainer/coaches for running, lifting, and other training programs
- Free ad-supported tier plus monetization/onboarding strategy
